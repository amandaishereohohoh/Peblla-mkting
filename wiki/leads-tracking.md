# Promotion & Leads Tracking

> Source: [Feishu Wiki — Promotion & Leads Tracking](https://riverroad.feishu.cn/wiki/ULBPw4tuCisygDksSM3cAwvinHh)

## Marketing Tech Stack

### Brevo (Email Campaign Platform)
- Primary tool for email campaigns
- Metrics: Open rate, click rate, bounce rate, unsubscribe rate
- Supports behavioral automation (follow-ups, conversion reminders)

### HubSpot (CRM + Lead Management)
- Central hub for ALL lead data (website forms, event signups, ad leads, content downloads)
- Features: Contact profiles, lead scoring, behavior tracking, interaction history
- Core platform for measuring lead acquisition effectiveness

### Google Search Console + SEMrush + Framer
- **GSC**: Search impressions, clicks, keyword performance, technical SEO issues
- **SEMrush**: Keyword tracking, competitor analysis, content gap identification, technical audits
- **Framer**: Basic page visit data (less accurate than GSC)

### Google Analytics (GA4)
- Core web analytics: source/medium, page dwell time, conversion paths, bounce rate

### Facebook Ads
- Current: Manual lead classification (filled form → qualified/unqualified → converted/unresponsive)
- **Planned improvement**: Migrate Facebook forms to Peblla website forms for automatic CRM sync
