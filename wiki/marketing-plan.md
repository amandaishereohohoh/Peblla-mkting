# Peblla Marketing Plan

> Source: [Feishu Wiki — Peblla Marketing Plan](https://riverroad.feishu.cn/wiki/EZHVw6ickiqN1skejizc5UQynIg)

## Social Media Operations

### LinkedIn
- **Frequency**: 2x/week
- **Content**: "Welcome to Peblla Family" series (new signups), product/website updates, tradeshow coverage

### Instagram & Facebook
- **Frequency**: 1-2x/week (synced)
- **Content**: LinkedIn highlights, client restaurant promos, holiday marketing tips

### YouTube
- **Frequency**: 1x/month (quality-focused)
- **Series**: Product promos, Peblla Academy tutorials, restaurant case studies
- **Cross-post**: Sync to blog + social media

### TikTok
- Currently outsourced, limited ROI — deprioritized

### SEO & Sales Enablement
- Website keyword optimization synced with Sales Enablement
- Target: 5 blogs/week on restaurant tech trends

## Social Media Management Services

### Tier 1: In-house for Clients
- **Pricing**: $299/store/month (2 posts/week) or $499/month bundle (2 stores)
- **Workflow**: Asset collection → Content production → 48hr client review → Publish
- **Metrics**: Monthly performance reports (reach, engagement, follower growth)

### Tier 2: Agency Partnership (二麦文化)
- **Pricing**: $349/month (2 posts + 1 reel)
- **Add-on**: +$150/month for viral video script

### Growth Roadmap
1. **Phase 1 (Current)**: Existing assets + client self-filming
2. **Phase 2 (6 months)**: Basic filming equipment, quarterly on-site shoots (+$200/month)
3. **Phase 3 (1 year)**: Local filming team, full-service management ($799+/month)
