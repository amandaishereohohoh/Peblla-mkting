## Key Sections

### Brand (`brand/`)
Peblla brand identity, UI design system with complete color token reference, hardware-to-SUNMI product mapping, and Figma design draft links for all product lines.

### Team (`team/`)
Virtual team structure with 13 specialized roles led by Sigma. Includes detailed role definitions, OKR framework aligned to inquiry conversion, dual-timezone reporting cadence, and the two-gate content review workflow (Devil's Advocate + Safer).

### Workflows (`workflows/`)
End-to-end process documentation for marketing kit production, business cards, video production (with Rex/Coco/Ace methodologies), SEO content publishing via Framer, social media operations, YouTube channel management, and private dashboard hosting.

### Methodology (`methodology/`)
Reusable standards: video script template structure, mandatory reference-image-based generation rules, and per-role content review checklists.

### Wiki (`wiki/`)
Feishu Wiki knowledge synced from the company knowledge base. Includes Sales Enablement materials catalog, marketing plan, leads tracking tech stack, strategic situation report, and team onboarding guide.

### Memories (`memories/`)
Full snapshot of all accumulated knowledge from the Peblla Marketing group chat session. Auto-synced daily to ensure the latest learnings, decisions, and team rules are preserved.
